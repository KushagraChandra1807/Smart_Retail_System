Smart Retail Assistant Knowledge Base

Return Policy:
Customers can return products within 30 days of purchase if the product is unused, undamaged, and has the original bill. Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days.

Inventory Policy:
If stock level falls below the minimum threshold, the system should generate a low stock alert. Store managers should reorder products based on demand forecast and recent sales trend.

Demand Forecasting Policy:
Demand forecasting helps predict future weekly sales using historical sales, holidays, promotions, weather, and economic indicators.

Anomaly Detection Policy:
Anomalies are unusual sales patterns such as sudden sales spikes, unexpected drops, or abnormal department behavior. These should be reviewed by managers.

Customer Support Policy:
Customer questions about products, returns, inventory, and store operations should be answered using verified retail policy data.

Low Stock Rule:
If available stock is lower than expected weekly demand, the system should recommend restocking.

Promotion Policy:
Discounts and promotional markdowns can increase sales during holiday weeks and seasonal demand periods.
